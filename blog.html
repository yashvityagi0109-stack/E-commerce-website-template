Art Behind Oxidised Jewellery: From Metal to Masterpiece

Every oxidised jewellery piece is a blend of art and chemistry. The process begins with base metals like silver, copper, or brass, which are oxidised using a controlled exposure to oxygen and sulfur. This forms a natural blackened surface, giving the jewellery its vintage, antique appearance.
